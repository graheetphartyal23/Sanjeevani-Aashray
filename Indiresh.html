About Us

A multi-specialty and superspeciality hospital in Patel Nagar, Dehradun. It offers a variety of services, including dermatology, pediatrics, orthopedics, gynecology, dentistry, ENT, and physiotherapy. The hospital is known for its commitment to patient safety and quality standards. It is accredited by the National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers (NABH) and certified by the National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories (NABL).

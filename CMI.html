About Us

CMI is the first multi speciality 150 Bedded hospital of Dehradun. The hospital apart from providing primary health services like family medicine, maternal & childcare, immunisation and nutrition also provides secondary health services like internal medicine, general surgery, obstetrics and gynaecology, paediatrics, ophthalmology, orthopaedics, dermatology & dentistry.

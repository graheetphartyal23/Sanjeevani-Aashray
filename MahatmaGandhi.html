About Us

Mahatma Gandhi Hospital (MGH), inaugurated on October 2, 2000, is a 1,400-bed multi-specialty teaching hospital. It provides extensive clinical teaching resources for medical, dental, and nursing students, supported by top-notch infrastructure, advanced medical equipment, and skilled medical professionals. MGH features a central diagnostic lab in biochemistry, microbiology, and pathology, and is recognized for its services in Advanced Reproductive Techniques (ART), medical and surgical endoscopy, and modern blood banking. The hospital also operates a Regional Emergency and Trauma Center, approved for medico-legal cases, equipped with advanced diagnostics, intensive care, and emergency facilities to handle road traffic accidents and forensic cases.

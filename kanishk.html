About Us

KANISHK Surgical and Super Speciality Hospital is a NABH accredited certified hospital, located centrally in Dehradun, capital of Uttarakhand (India). It has been established in the year of March 2011. It is one of the most prestigious hospital of Uttarakhand providing world class health care services in the field of Advance Laparoscopy, Surgical Gastroenterology, Medical Gastroenterology, Obstetrics and Genecology, General Surgery, Oncology & Palliative Care, Orthopedics, Joint Replacement & Sports Injury, Neurosurgery, General Medicine, Chest Medicine, Nephrology and Hemodialysis, Urology, Aesthesia & Pain Management, Critical Care, Nutritional Healthcare, Radiology, Pathology, Cardiology, Physiotherapy & Medical Rehabilitation.

About Us

Welcome to Arihant Hospital, a beacon of healthcare excellence and compassion that has been serving the community for the past 20 years. Established with the vision to provide outstanding medical care with a human touch, Arihant Hospital has grown to become one of the most trusted names in healthcare, not just in our local community but across the nation and beyond. Our journey began two decades ago with a simple yet profound mission: to offer patient-centered healthcare that combines the best of medical expertise, technology, and compassion. Over the years, we have steadfastly adhered to this mission, evolving into a multi-specialty healthcare institution that caters to a wide array of medical needs.
